A Girl Named Tex

Forged in the DFW region of Texas and raised on Visual Studio and Counter-Strike, Lana Cagle is a Computer Science BA in her Junior year of college at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. Her work ranges across many forms, from full-stack development to artistic endeavors.

If you're here, you probably are interested in the various projects I work on, but also included on this site is a collection of most of my artworks, as well as some other random stuff if you really care.

Who the hell is 3HOTL?

3HOTL isn't really a specific thing. It's a group of artists, it's a name to play games under, it's a tagline found hidden in corners around America. What does it stand for? That's on a need to know basis, and you don't need to know.
